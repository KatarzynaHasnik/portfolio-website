My Story

My name is Katarzyna. I am a highly motivated, determined and creative UX/UI Designer with visual graphic and research skills and work experience. I have a passion for pixel-perfect precision, Human-Centred Design, eye-catching visual user and market quality aesthetics with 16+ years of experience combine together in SaaS, IaaS, PaaS with a passion for FinTech, InsurTech, EdTech and GreenTech and excessive experience in Public Medical and Holistic Health, E-Commerce, Suitable Fashion, Tourism, Logistic and MotorTech. I am naturally a great team player with leadership skills and experiences and high motivation to help solve problems by providing meaningful solutions to our users and clients. I have the ability to bring everything I have learned over the years to User Experience by applying skills of researching target audience, interacting with customers and clients, conducting, testing, interviewing users and prototyping wireframes. My intuitive and creative understanding of visual design, high standard problem-solving skills are my professional assets that lead to well-balanced excellence. Because of my positive mindset, I can cope with time pressure and work with the highest performance to meet deadlines. I have strong communication, leadership, and interpersonal skills and I am an excellent team player. Your company will surely benefit from my creative imagination, education, knowledge, tech UI Design, and UX Design skills.
